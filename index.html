Visit Portland

Enjoy art, culture and sports in this vibrant and self-proclaimed "weird" city.

Top three activities to do in Portland

Attend the Big Float or float with friends on any of several rivers to choose from.

Whether you float down river or float in place, Portland has the best places for busting out floaties.

Attend sporting events, concerts, and Comic Cons at the Moda Center.

The Moda Center is the center for cultural fun and a great place to experience Portland's shows and games.

Enjoy the wide variety of cuisines and street art faires that Portland has to offer.

Hungry for unique foods? Portland has it all! Hungry for art? Portland has even more!
